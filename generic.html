**OnlineStore2: An E-Shop Web Application Powered by ASP.NET Framework**

Introduction:
OnlineStore2 is a repository for an e-shop web application developed using the ASP.NET Framework. This project serves as a simplified version of , offering essential features in a compact package. The future of OnlineStore2 will be similar to popular e-commerce platforms like NopCommerce so then you can create your own online store and start selling products with ease.
Here is a list of the current key features provided by OnlineStore2, followed by the planned modifications and additional features for the future:
Current Key Features:
1. Product Management:
- Addition, editing, and removal of products from your online store.
- Convenient organization of products into categories and subcategories for easy navigation.
2. Shopping Cart and Checkout:
- Shopping experience with a user-friendly cart and checkout page.
3. Customer Registration and Authentication:
- Simple registration process for customers to create accounts.
- Secure login functionality, ensuring the privacy and protection of customer information.
Future Key Features:
1. Shopping Cart and Checkout:
- Shopping experience with a user-friendly cart system.
- Secure checkout process, allowing customers to finalize their purchases with confidence.
2. Payment Integration:
- Integration with trusted payment gateways, enabling smooth and secure transactions.
- Support for popular payment methods, providing convenience for customers.
3. Order Management:
- Comprehensive order tracking system, allowing you to monitor and manage orders efficiently.
- Real-time notifications for order updates, ensuring smooth communication with customers.
4. Responsive Design:
- Mobile-friendly interface, ensuring a seamless shopping experience on various devices.
Conclusion:
OnlineStore2 e-shop web application built on the ASP.NET Framework. The completed version of OnlineStore2 with its essential features for product management, shopping cart functionality, checkout, and order management, it will provide a solid foundation for creating your own online store. Whether you are starting a small business or expanding your existing operations, OnlineStore2 offers a simplified e-commerce solution to kickstart your online selling journey.
